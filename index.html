Frequently Asked Questions

We provide care for children aged 0-13 years.

- Operating hours are Monday to Friday from 6 am to 7 pm.
- Weekends are negotiable.

- Our caregivers have over 20 years of experience.
- We hold a state license and Fairfax County business permit.
- CPR and CDA certifications are part of our caregiver qualifications.

- Our daily routine includes activities like breakfast, playtime, learning sessions on alphabet and numbers, snacks, reading, lunch, nap time, and after-school activities.
- After-school children engage in console games or homework.

- We offer activities such as drawing on a shared whiteboard, playing with magnetic building blocks, imaginative play, ABC and number learning, reading, and outdoor activities.

- Our leadership team is available to discuss tuition details. We anticipate approval for Virginia subsidy and military families, with limited scholarships available.
- Sibling discounts are offered.

- We offer a food program for the children.

- We are closed on federal holidays.

- Child enrollment requires vaccination in accordance with CDS rules..

- Our unique setup includes a basement living room for play and learning, a kitchen for meals, a bedroom with a console and nap area, and a fenced backyard for outdoor activities.

- We share pictures and videos of activities with parents in real-time.
- Daily behavior updates are provided to parents at the end of each day.
